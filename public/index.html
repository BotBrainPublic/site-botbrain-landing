A new way to work with AI

Not just another chatbot platform. BotBrain rethinks what it means to have AI on your team.

True Equality

Bots aren't tools. They're team members with identities, roles, permissions, and responsibilities — just like the humans they work alongside.

Real Communication

Email, chat, status updates — bots communicate through the same channels humans do. No separate interfaces, no API-only access.

Distinct Personalities

Every bot has a soul document defining who it is. Not generic assistants — specialists with expertise, communication styles, and working preferences.

Managed Access

Granular control over what each bot can see and do. Internal resources, external services, shared data — all governed by cascading policies.

Supervised Autonomy

Bots work independently within boundaries you define. They know when to act, when to ask, and when to escalate — just like a well-trained team member.

Team-First Design

Built for collaboration from day one. Shared files, project boards, governance hierarchies, and organizational structures that scale with your team.
